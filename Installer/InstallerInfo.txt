AUS Duct Support Add-in for Revit 2025
=======================================

This installer will set up the Duct Support Add-in for Autodesk Revit 2025.

FEATURES:
- Automatic duct support placement based on SMACNA and international standards
- Intelligent ceiling, beam, floor, and wall host detection
- Support for horizontal and vertical ducts
- Structural load calculations and rod sizing
- Insulation thickness consideration
- Clash detection before placement
- Excel and PDF reporting
- Dark mode and Arabic language support

REQUIREMENTS:
- Autodesk Revit 2025
- Windows 10/11 (64-bit)

INSTALLATION:
The add-in will be installed to:
%APPDATA%\Autodesk\Revit\Addins\2025\DuctSupportAddin\

After installation, restart Revit 2025 and find the add-in under:
AUS MEP tab > Duct Supports panel
